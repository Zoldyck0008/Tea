what makes our bubble tea special?

Bubble tea is a Taiwanese recipe made by blending a tea base with milk, fruit and fruit juices, then adding the signature “bubbles” - yummy tapioca pearls that sit at the bottom.

It is a tea base, often time sweetened and combined with milk or fruit flavorings. What makes bubble tea stand out is the fact that it contains chewy bubble – tapioca pearls. They change the texture of the whole beverage, making drinking bubble tea a whole new experience.

Do You Eat The Pearls In Bubble Tea? by admin / 14th november, 2023

The short answer to this question is yes. The pearls in bubble tea are entirely edible and safe to eat, and are what make drinking bubble tea a totally unique experience.

In fact, it’s important to remember that you should always chew the pearls in bubble tea before swallowing them. This largely comes down to the fact that whole pearls are more difficult and can take longer for your stomach to digest.

This is why your bubble tea is served with a thick straw that allows you to drink your bubble tea and chew the delightful bubble tea pearls that add a burst of flavour simultaneously.

How Are Bubble Tea Pearls Made? by admin / 14th november, 2023

The pearls in bubble tea, also known as tapioca pearls, are made from tapioca starch, an extract of the South American cassava plant. To make the pearls, boiling water is added to the starch and kneaded to form a dough-like texture. The dough is then rolled and cut into small pearl shapes and added into a boiling mixture of brown sugar and water to create the end product.

On the other hand, popping bubbles are made from water, fruit juice, sugar, and plant-based Alginic acid, which also makes them safe to eat. Popping bubbles are made using a spherification process. Tapioca pearls are gentle on the stomach and easy to digest.

How Many Calories Are There In Bubble Tea? by admin / 14th november, 2023

There’s no denying that bubble tea can be an indulgent treat. However, as there are so many variations, it can be tricky to know how many calories you’re consuming when you drink it.

That said, the answer to this question largely depends on the type of bubble tea in question among other factors. For instance, while 490 ml of brown sugar milk bubble tea contains around 270 calories, fruit bubble tea contains approximately 220 calories, though this can differ from store to store. As such, the type of bubble tea, alongside various other factors can impact the calorie content of your bubble tea.
